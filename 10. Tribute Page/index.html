Dhirubhai Ambani: The Polyester Prince

Here,s a time line of Dhirubhai Ambani's life:

  • 1932 - Born in Gujrat, India.
  • 1948 - At the of 16, left for work in A. Besse and Co. in Port to Aden, Yemen.
  • 1955 - Tied the knot with Kokilaben.
  • 1958 - Founded Reliance Industries.
  • 1966 - Made his debut in backward integration and opened the Reliance Textile mill.
  • 1977 - Became the first face of Indian business who conquered the Industrial sector globally.
  • 1986 - Paralyzed his right hand and was in coma for more than a week and a number of doctors were consulted.
  • 2002 - Died at the age of 70.
  • 2012 - Reliance Industries became the first-ever privately-owned Indian Company enlisted in the illustrious list of Fortune 500.
  • 2016 - Honoured with the 2nd highest civilian award, Padma Vibhushan after many years post his death for his excellent contribution to the Indian market and its economy.
